Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how NoteApp ("the App") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our service.

Developer Information

  • App Name: NoteApp
  • Developer: iwamaki
  • Contact: noteapp@iwamaki.app

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Note Data: Your notes, categories, and tags are stored only on your device
  • Settings: App preferences and settings are stored only on your device

Information Sent to Our Server (Optional)

  • Error Reports: Crash and error information (app version, device type, OS version) to help us improve the app
  • Feedback: Content you voluntarily submit through the feedback feature
  • Device Identifier: An anonymous identifier (UUID format) that does not contain any personally identifiable information

AI Features (Optional, When Available)

  • Note Content: If AI features are enabled and you choose to use them, your note content may be sent to AI service providers for processing
  • This only occurs when you explicitly initiate an AI-powered action
  • AI features require a Google account sign-in when available

Payment Information

  • This App is a paid app purchased through Google Play
  • We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information
  • All payment processing is handled by Google Play

How We Use Your Information

  • Providing core app functionality (note management, categories, tags)
  • Improving app quality and fixing bugs
  • Responding to user support requests

Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When you explicitly use AI features (if available), your content is processed by AI service providers
  • When required by law or legal process

Data Security

  • Your note data is stored locally on your device
  • Authentication credentials are stored securely with encryption
  • All communications with our server use HTTPS encryption

Your Rights

  • Data Deletion: You can delete all your data from within the app settings
  • Data Export: You can export your notes as a ZIP file
  • Contact Us: Reach out to noteapp@iwamaki.app for any data-related requests

Children's Privacy

This App is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are under 18, please do not use this App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app.
