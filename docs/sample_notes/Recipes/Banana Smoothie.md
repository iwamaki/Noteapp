# Banana Smoothie

Quick and healthy breakfast option.

## Ingredients

- 1 Banana (frozen)
- 200ml Milk
- 1 tbsp Honey
- 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
- Ice cubes

## Steps

1. Add all ingredients to blender
2. Blend until smooth
3. Pour into glass
4. Enjoy immediately!

## Variations

- **Protein boost:** Add 1 scoop protein powder
- **Green version:** Add spinach
- **Chocolate:** Add 1 tbsp cocoa powder

---

*Prep time: 5 minutes*
