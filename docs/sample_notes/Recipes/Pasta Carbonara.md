# Pasta Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish.

## Ingredients

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Spaghetti | 200g |
| Guanciale | 100g |
| Egg yolks | 3 |
| Parmesan | 50g |
| Black pepper | to taste |

## Instructions

1. Boil pasta in salted water
2. Cut guanciale into small pieces
3. Fry guanciale until crispy
4. Mix egg yolks with grated parmesan
5. Combine hot pasta with guanciale
6. Remove from heat, add egg mixture
7. Toss quickly and serve

## Tips

> The key is to remove the pan from heat before adding the egg mixture to avoid scrambling.

**Cooking time:** 20 minutes
**Servings:** 2
